Sikkim is a state in the north-eastern part of India. The capital of Sikkim is Gangtok. It is a nice city. People in Sikkim speak Nepali, Lepcha, Bhutia, Limbu and English. Sikkim has hilly areas and rocky slopes. People use terrace farming. They get water from the mountain streams. People grow tea, cardamom, ginger, apples and oranges. The forest regions of Sikkim have a wide variety of plants, birds and animals. Pine and oak trees, and bamboo and banana grow in plenty. We find different kinds of orchids.
